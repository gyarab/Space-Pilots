Game



Story



You were awakened in chaos and confusion. Enemy fleet is about to arrive to your home planet. You always knew they might attack, but you never expected them to do so quickly.

To your dismay, you have found out that reconnaissance satellites have detected huge amount of enemy space ships approaching to your planet. Your only chance is to stop them before arriving.

Most of the fleet of your army has been destroyed, enemy fleet was damaged, but not destroyed. You and your ship is the only thing that can prevent destruction of your home.
